Project

Virtual Cursor Gesticulation by Hand Gestures and Speech Interaction

Abstract

Our project's main objective is to revolutionize the way we interect with computers by introducing a virtual cursor controlled solely through hand gestures and speech recognition.
This advanced system is designed to smoothly perform all mouse and keyboard functions,eliminating the requirement for extra hardware. By utilizing just a webcam and a microphone,
our virtual cursor surpasses the limitations of conventional input devices, making computer interaction more intuitive and accessible. It simply redifines the traditional mouse and
keyboard usage through hand gestures and voice commands.

Problem Statement

Our project "Virtual Cursor Gesticulation by Hand Gestures and Speech Interaction" redifines traditional mouse usage by combining computer vision for hand gestures and speech recognition.
Using intuitive hand movements, users can perform actions like clicking and scrolling. Additionally,speech commands offer a hands-free alternative for seamless interaction.
While traditional input devices have limitations,our goal is not only to overcome these challenges but also to provide users with a more intuitive and efficient means of interaction.
By introducing speech recognition into the mix,we extend the possibilities,allowing users to control their screens through both gestures and voice commands,
creating a versatile and user-friendly interface.
